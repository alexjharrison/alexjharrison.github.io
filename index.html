About Me

Hi there! My name’s Alex Harrison and I am set out to become a full stack web developer. Ever since I was a wee lad, I’ve been hacking away at computers. In the early days I slayed the DOS commands on my grandmother’s Apple IIe and read files from those five inch floppy drives. Everybody remembers the 3” disks taking the A:/ drive but who remembers the 5” B:/ drive? 👉This guy 👈

I’ve had experience in programming in such languages as Java, C++ , regular ‘ol C, Assembler for an 8086 processor, VB functions for Excel, and most recently C#. I know enough of these languages to say hello to the world and do some basic calculation functions but not near enough to get paid to do stuff. Also, I am insane and have played through many programming style games such as SpaceChem, TIS-100, Shenzhen IO, Uplink and HackNet. Proof of insanity
